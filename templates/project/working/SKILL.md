# SKILL: working

- **Role:** work-in-progress (optional)
- **Mutability:** MUTABLE / disposable.
- **Contains:** drafts, scratch and intermediate material that is not yet
  canonical (`sources/primary/`), not background (`sources/secondary/`), and not a
  finished artifact (`output/`).
- **Purpose / how to use:** a staging area. Nothing here is authoritative; it can
  be regenerated or discarded. Promote material to `sources/primary/` when it
  becomes canonical, or to `output/` when final.
- **Provenance:** transient; created during authoring and iteration.
