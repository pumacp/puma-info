# SKILL: sources/secondary

- **Role:** secondary source-of-truth (accumulated knowledge / context)
- **Mutability:** IMMUTABLE — never modified; a fixed record of prior research and work.
- **Contains:** the accumulated knowledge from the project's earlier phases —
  notes, research, prior context, historical material. Optional phase subfolders
  (`01-…/`, `02-…/`) are natural here.
- **Purpose / how to use:** read-only **context** for generation and for the
  author when correcting the primary. Agents may read it to ground output; they
  must **not** modify it.
- **Provenance:** produced during the project's research phases; feeds the primary
  (via the author, outside the pipeline) and is available as context when
  generating derived material.
